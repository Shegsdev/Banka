    How can I open an account?

    Here are the steps to follow:

    • Visit the homepage by clicking this link.
    • Click the Login button at the top right corner.
    • Enter your email and password or navigate to the sign up page to register if haven't already.
    • After successfully registration navigate back to the homepage.
    • Scroll down to the middle of the page and click the get started button.
    • Fill in your details in the form on the new page and submit.

    Can my account be dormant?

    Yes, your account can be dormant if not used.
